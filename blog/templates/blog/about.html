About

This product is 100% free with no limitations on how many times you can use it!

Its purpose is to teach everyone vocabulary through a series of games such as flashcards, word-jumble, and word guessing!

We believe that everyone should have an equal and fair chance at learning and that resources like this should NOT be behind a paywall!

Teachers will have a group created for them when they sign up. Students will then be able to join that teachers group (through the teacher-lookup page) and view all the word list and test that the teacher creates.

The teacher and will be able to track the test scores of ALL students within their group!

Students will also be able to view their own scores so they know what they have to work on

Features

some features are but are not limited to

  • Word-list creation
  • Set custom definitions/sentences
  • Play games to study the words within the wordlist
  • Create test
  • Create and customize printable worksheets


Feel free to send us any suggestions you may have for the site!
